Upfront and transparent pricing to help you grow

  • Ramping Up Management
    For our standard EOR, daily management, and payroll services
    • We charge a simple monthly management fee based on your employee compensation
    • Team of fewer than 20 people: 12-15% management fee based on your employee compensation
    • Special Offer Team of more than 20 people: 10% management fee based on your employee compensation
  • New Employee Hiring
    Charged only if we helped hire a new employee
    • One-time fee
    • One month base compensation of the hired employee for employee annual compensation less than USD 50,000
    • 20% of base compensation of the hired employee for employee annual compensation equal to or more than USD 50,000
    * If only using hiring service (no EOR, payroll, management services), all recruiting services are 20% annual compensation of hired candidate.
